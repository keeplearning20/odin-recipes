How to make Tea.

Description

Who doesn't love a good tea? Well, I guess a lot of people prefer coffee. But I like both. Especially, tea with milk.

It's easy to make. It's not only a warm beverage to soothe you, it can also be a quick snack when paired a few biscuits!

Ingredients

  • milk
  • tea leafs
  • sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Put some water in a kettle, and turn it on.
  2. Add the tea leafs.
  3. Once the water starts boiling, add half a cup of milk.
  4. Let it boil for another 2 minutes.
  5. Turn off the kettle, and pour your delicious tea!
