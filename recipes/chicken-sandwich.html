Chicken Sandwich Recipe.

Description

A Chicken Sandwich is incredibly easy to make. It's very healthy and tasy. Thus, I have started making it as a part of my latest fasting diet.

In this page, I will show you the ingredient, and the steps required to make a delicious chicken sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mayonese
  • Boiled Eggs

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken breast.
  2. Boil an egg.
  3. Mash the boiled egg into a bowl.
  4. Add 1 table spoon Mayonese into it.
  5. Add salt and pepper, and keep stirring.
  6. Add boiled chicken breast pieces into this paste, and stir some more.
  7. Toast a couple of slices of bread.
  8. Put the paste into the toast, and tada! Enjoy!
